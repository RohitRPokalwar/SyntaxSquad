Electrical Appliances for Beginners

This comprehensive course introduces beginners to the world of electrical appliances, covering fundamental concepts, safety measures, and practical applications. Designed by expert instructor N. Bagade, this program provides hands-on knowledge about common household and industrial electrical appliances, their working principles, maintenance, and troubleshooting.

Course Highlights

  • Understanding basic electrical concepts and terminology
  • Safety precautions when working with electrical appliances
  • Common household appliances: operation and maintenance
  • Troubleshooting basic electrical problems
  • Energy efficiency and smart appliances
  • Practical demonstrations and hands-on exercises

Whether you're a homeowner looking to understand your appliances better, a beginner interested in electrical systems, or someone considering a career in appliance repair, this course provides the perfect foundation. No prior electrical knowledge is required - we start from the very basics!
